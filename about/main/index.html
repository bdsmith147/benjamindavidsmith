Faith

I am a disciple of Jesus Christ. I find hope, encouragement, and healing in His restored Gospel. My faith in God the Father and Jesus Christ is founded on what I’ve been taught and on what I have learned and know of myself. My living faith distills from the choice I make to “let God prevail” in my life 1. As a member of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints, I strive to love and serve all others as my neighbors (Luke 10:25-37). I am still a work in progress, and I am grateful for the people around me who let me try again when I fail

On this website, I will occassionally post my thoughts, feelings, and reflections on these and other topics.
